What is NBaller ?

It's a mix between a fantasy league and a card game. Show that you are the strongest by choosing the NBA player who will make the best score #NBL of the night. If this one is a TOP 10 of the night then you win a unique animated card of your player with his stats of the night. The goal is to collect the most cards to impress the world.

To ensure that your card is unique, it will be integrated into the NFT etherum system. This card will belong to you entirely and you will be free to exchange it, resell it, or just flex.

How NBA points has been calculed

#NBL points is calculed every day according to the performance of the players: his shots, points, rebounds, passes, steals, blocks and even his lost balls. And since a player is nothing without his team, a win/loss bonus will be applied to the final score
