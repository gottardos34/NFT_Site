What is NBaller ?

It's a mix between a fantasy league and a NTF collection. Show that you are the strongest by choosing the NBA player who will make the best score #NBL of the night. If the player is a TOP 10 of the night then a unique animated card of your player with his stats of the night will be created.

To ensure that your card is unique, it will be integrated into the NFT etherum system. All the cards will be dropped at the end of the season. You will be able to sell, buy or trade cards on the OpenSea platform. Follow us on discord, instagram or open sea

How NBA points has been calculed

#NBL points is calculed every day according to the performance of the players: his shots, points, rebounds, passes, steals, blocks and even his lost balls. And since a player is nothing without his team, a win/loss bonus will be applied to the final score
